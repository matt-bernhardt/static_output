We help MIT faculty and researchers manage, store, and share data they produce.<\/p>\n

Assistance with creating data management plans<\/h2>\n

Many research funders<\/a> have requirements for data sharing and data management plans<\/a>. We can help you to create these plans, assess the data management needs of your project, and help you to identify data management solutions.<\/p>\n

Individual consultation<\/h2>\n

We are available to help you, your lab, or center understand your data management needs and recommend best practices for keeping your data usable, now and into the future.<\/p>\n

Virtual drop-in help hours<\/h2>\n

During the Fall and Spring semesters, we will hold virtual help hours via Zoom. No appointment necessary. Simply log into the posted Zoom room during designated help hours to talk to one of our data management staff. Note, MIT touchstone authentication is required to join.<\/p>\n
