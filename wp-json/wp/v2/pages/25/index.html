Increase your research impact
\n<\/strong>Making your data available to other researchers can impact discovery and relevance of your research.<\/span><\/p>\n

Save time<\/strong>
\nPlanning ahead<\/a> for your data management needs will save you time and resources.<\/p>\n

Preserve your data<\/strong>
\nDepositing your data in a repository<\/a> safeguards your investment of time and resources while preserving your research contribution for you and others to use.<\/p>\n

Maintain data integrity<\/strong>
\nManaging and documenting your data<\/a>\u00a0throughout its life cycle will allow you and others to understand and use your data in the future.<\/p>\n

Meet grant requirements<\/strong>
\nMany funding agencies<\/a> now require that researchers deposit data collected as part of a research project.<\/p>\n

Promote new discoveries<\/strong>
\nSharing your data<\/a> with other researchers can lead to new and unanticipated discoveries and provide research material for those with little or no funding.<\/p>\n

Support open access<\/strong>
\n<\/span>Be a catalyst for research and discovery. Show your support for open access by sharing your data.<\/p>\n","protected":false},"excerpt":{"rendered":"
